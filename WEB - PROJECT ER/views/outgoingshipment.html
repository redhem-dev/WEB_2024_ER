How to use ?

If you want to remove a product from the inventory, you can do so after checking the checkbox on top of the form. Different Form will open and it will allow you to remove your product.

It is not your responsibility to subtract the current inventory with the new shipment, just to input number of units of product that are abandoning the inventory. Rest will be taken care of by the system.
